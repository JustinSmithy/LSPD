FAQ

How the LSPD divisional system works.

How do I get access to the portal?
Submit a divisional application. Once an admin approves it, register an officer account with the email you used. Your account is then verified and you gain dashboard access.
What is a Transport / Trafficking Arrest?
Used when you arrest someone for Transporting Illegal Goods but no drugs or firearms were physically recovered — for example the goods were already delivered or disposed of. Choose Trafficking Arrest (No Seizure) as the log type, add the arrested person, and make sure Transporting Illegal Goods is one of their charges. No normal seizure is required. Items show as "Contraband / Undisclosed Goods" on public records.
How do I log an arrest or seizure?
Dashboard → Log Activity. Choose the type, add seized items (street value totals automatically), set the location, then add arrested persons with their charges and evidence. Submit and it appears on the records list.
How do mugshots work?
When you log an arrest, the system matches the name to an existing suspect file and attaches the mugshot. You can also add suspects manually in the Suspects tab and upload a photo.
Who is an admin?
The first officer to register becomes admin automatically. Admins approve applications, verify officers, and can promote others.
