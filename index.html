FAQ

How LSPD Operations works.

How do I get portal access?
Submit a divisional application. An admin approves it, then you register an officer account with the same email and get verified for dashboard access.
How do I log an arrest or seizure?
Dashboard → Log Activity. Choose Arrest, a seizure type, or both. Add charges, location, evidence and arrested persons, then submit. Records appear immediately and feed the leaderboard and analysis.
What is the Command Log?
Supervisors and command staff record Off-Duty notices, Reassignments, and High-Speed pursuit reports. It is only visible to Sergeant rank and above (and admins).
How does the leaderboard work?
Officers are ranked by a weighted activity score across logs filed, arrests made, and value seized.
Where is the Vice Unit?
Vice is a separate division with its own portal at vice-unit.com.
